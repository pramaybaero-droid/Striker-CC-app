Claim community ownership

Use this page once, right after the default IISc community is created and you've signed in to the app and created your player. It promotes your player to owner, which unlocks Admin mode inside the app.

Default: iisc-carrom-club. For other communities, use that community's slug.

This only works while the community has no owner yet. After promotion, return to the app, sign out and sign back in to refresh your role, then tap Admin in the top bar.
